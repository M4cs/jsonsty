JSONsty

A fast, free, and secure way to store JSON data in the cloud.

JSONsty offers a free solution for small scale projects to store data simply. We offer a REST API and web panel dashboard that will help you manage your data more effeciently. Use JSONsty as your next Development Database or a small scale cacheing database for your mobile application. With the services we offer, abilities are endless for storing your data... free!
